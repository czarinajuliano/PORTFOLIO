September Collection is a multi-brand retail store selling a wide variety of goods with a collaboration of both establishes and emerging online brands. A lifestyle fasion concept store which focuses on redefining online shopping in iloilo city. This project consist of mock-up design of September Collection system. The system consists of three parts the brand-partners, cashier and the owner. The brandpartners consist of net income, product listing and pending items while for the cashier it has sales, inventory and pending items also. For the owner sales, pending, inventory, net income.
