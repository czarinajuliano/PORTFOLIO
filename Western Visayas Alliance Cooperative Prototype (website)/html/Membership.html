MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION GUIDELINES

Who Are Qualified to Join?

Open to all Primary Cooperatives operating in Western Visayas.

,

Membership Fee: Php1000.00

Advantages of Being a Member

Access to the Federation Affairs, Financial, Business Development, Training, Consultancy, Info-Technologies, Marketing, Legal and Linkages Services of the Federation.

Right of participation in the Governance of the federation.

Documentary Requirements

Duly Accomplished membership application form;

Letter of Intent to Join WVAC

Board Resolution on Direct Membershio to WVAC willfully & unconditionally

Copies of the following Documents:

CDA Registration

Articles of Cooperation & Bylaws

Amendments if any

Latest Audited FS

List of Officer and Management & Staff

Required Share Capital

The Applicant shall subscribe for atleast 40 shares valued at Php1000.00 each. At least 25% of the subscribes shares shall be paid upon application. However no member shall own or hold more than 10% of the total paid up of WVAC.

Duties & Responsibilities

Pay the required membership fee and required share capital;

Remit CETF to WVAC

Pay other dues as may determined by the Board and the GA;

Patronize the Federations products, programs and services;

Attend to the Federations activities( meeting, forums, workshops, etc.);

Abide by the rules and regulations set forth in the WVAC by laws and policies, guidelines, decisions set by the board and the GA;

Submit annually audited FS, Annual Reports, and Data Form(DUF);

Respond to WVAC survey, forums, meetings and other related activities from time to time;

Ensure compliance with the standard imposed by WVAC, CDA and other government regulatory agencies.

Promote the aims and purposes of WVAC, the success of its services and programs, the welfare of its members and the cooperative movement as a whole.

CETF Policy

A primary affiliate which is not a member of any secodary or apex organizations shall remit 50% of the CETF allocation to WVAC, an amount equivalent to what is due to apex organization.

In case of primary affiliates which is an affiliate of other secondary and apex organization, such primary affiliate shall declare its CETF allocation to WVAC upon application of membership. Certificate of remittance shall be required.

All CETF Remitance shall be divided into:

    >
  • 50% WVAC Operation
  • 35% CETF Entrust
  • 15% Capital Build Up
